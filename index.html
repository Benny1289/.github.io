La Vie Nebulizer System UI and Control Devices This was a project proposed by my cousin and me to create a next-generation nebulizer, equipped with wireless connectivity for portability, and a better interface. The design uses Android Studio to create an app that is uploaded into a tablet; the tablet then is connected to a custom circuit board. The circuit board gathers information like the air quality, temperature, and humidity to forward to the tablet, which displays the information. The tablet also stops the system if a critical level is reached or the time limit is reached. The tablet also has a custom entertainment system, aimed toward kids, for playful use. The next next-generation nebulizer is being rolled out in the middle-east and authorized by the department of health for use in the United States. Currently, the product is being patented, and so I can not divulge any of the specifics like the circuitry or code for legal reasons until the process is complete. -Java
